Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

CatylstRx is committed to transparency about how information is handled on this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and what users should understand before using the site.

2. Intended Use / No PHI

This website is intended for clinician-facing informational and workflow support tools. Users should not enter protected health information (PHI), patient names, dates of birth, medical record numbers, or other patient-identifying information into the website or its tools unless the site explicitly states that such data is supported and protected.

3. Information We May Collect

We may collect limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referral source, and basic analytics information. This information may be used to operate, secure, and improve the site.

4. Tool Input Handling

Tool calculations and generated outputs are processed locally in the user's browser and are not transmitted to or stored by the website, unless explicitly stated otherwise. Based on the current codebase, the TaperCal tool runs client-side and does not submit its form inputs to a backend service.

5. Cookies and Analytics

The site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality and analytics. Users can control cookies through their browser settings. No dedicated client-side analytics script is configured in the current codebase, so any analytics-related collection would depend on hosting or infrastructure-level services rather than tool-form submissions.

6. How Information May Be Used

Information may be used to operate the website, understand usage patterns, improve tools and content, maintain security, and respond to user inquiries.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that support hosting, analytics, security, or site operations, as applicable.

8. Data Security

Reasonable measures may be used to protect site information, but no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Choices

Users can limit cookies through browser settings and may contact us with privacy-related questions.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: admin@catylstrx.com

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version of this page is marked with the following date: Last updated: March 2026.
