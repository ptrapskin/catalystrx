About

CatylstRx started as a small side project while I was experimenting with the recent “vibe coding” trend. The idea is simple: create apps or utilities I can’t find elsewhere that might make practice more efficient, safer, or more effective.

If others find them useful too, even better. I’ll add new tools as ideas or needs come up and as time allows.

I’m Philip Trapskin, PharmD, BCPS, a pharmacist with more than 25 years of experience in clinical practice and healthcare leadership.
