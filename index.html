Your own personal pharmacist.

CatylstRx is exploring a new model where each person or family can have one pharmacist assigned to them, someone who knows their medicines and helps them manage them over time.

A pharmacist-developed concept focused on better medicine help for patients and caregivers.

Instead of speaking to a different pharmacist each time you call the pharmacy, imagine having one pharmacist who is your consistent point of contact.

Someone who answers questions, helps coordinate refills, manages changes from doctors, connects you to adherence services, helps with insurance and Medicare drug coverage, and looks for ways to lower prescription costs. For caregivers, it means one person to call when something changes.

What this model could provide

One pharmacist who stays with you over time.

  • A dedicated pharmacist
    One pharmacist who knows your medicines and follows your care over time.
  • Help managing complex medicine routines
    Support when doses change, new medicines are added, or side effects appear.
  • Insurance and cost navigation
    Help understanding Medicare drug plans, prior authorizations, and savings options.
  • Support for caregivers
    A single point of contact for families helping manage medicines for parents or loved ones.

Why this exists

Many medicine problems happen when no one person is helping manage them.

Many medication problems happen because people do not have one person responsible for helping them manage their medicines. CatylstRx is exploring a model where a personal pharmacist fills that role.
